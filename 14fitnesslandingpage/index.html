Back pain is a common ailment that affects millions of people worldwide. Whether caused by poor posture, muscle strain, or underlying health conditions, back pain can significantly impact one’s quality of life. Fortunately, yoga offers a natural and effective way to alleviate back pain through specific asanas (postures) that strengthen, stretch, and align the spine and surrounding muscles. This blog explores how yoga asanas can be a powerful tool in managing and preventing back pain.
